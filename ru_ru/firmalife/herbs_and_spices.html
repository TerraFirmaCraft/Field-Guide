Травы и специи

In Firmalife, there are a number of small plants you can collect and grow on your own, some of which can be used for cooking. These are called herbs. These plants, once harvested, may be cultivated in a Greenhouse, in which they consume 20 points of N, P, and K.

Open to the next page to see information on all herb spawning locations and what they can be used for.

Структура

Basil spawns between 0-32°C and 100-500mm of groundwater.

Basil leaves, made from basil, are used in pizza.

Структура

Bay Laurel spawns between 10-32°C and 100-300mm of groundwater.

Bay laurel is chopped into bay leaves and used with cilantro to make flavorful rice in a pot.

Структура

Cardamom spawns between 17-24°C and 330-500mm of groundwater.

Ground Cardamom is made from grinding the cardamom plant, which is used to make Rice Pilaf.

Структура

Cilantro spawns between 10-24°C and 90-300mm of groundwater.

Cilantro can be chopped and used for salsa and used with bay leaves in a pot to make flavorful cooked rice.

Структура

Cumin spawns between 16-27°C and 100-300mm of groundwater.

Ground Cumin is made from grinding the cumin plant, which is used to make Carne Asada.

Структура

Oregano spawns between 20-27°C and 90-350mm of groundwater.

Oregano is used for lasagna.

Структура

Pimento spawns between 18-24°C and 200-400mm of groundwater.

Allspice is made from grinding the pimento plant, which is used to make Spiced Flour for use in Pumpkin Pie.

Структура

Vanilla spawns between 22-30°C and 350-500mm of groundwater.

Vanilla must be dried, and then can be made into ice cream.
