Minecarts

Minecarts are a means of transporting players, entities, and large blocks. Blocks that would normally overburden the player can be transported in minecarts. Large Vessels, Barrels, Powderkegs, Anvils, and Crucibles can be added to carts by pressing both Right Click and Shift. Using Shift with an empty hand removes the block. Forging minecart items can either be done with Iron or Steel, with the steel-based recipes being less expensive.
