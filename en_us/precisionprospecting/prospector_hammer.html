Prospector's Hammer

Prospector's Hammer

The Prospector's Hammer is a near copy of its pick-y brother, with the notable exception of a smaller range, only searching the 13x13x13 area centered on the clicked block.

A mold for the tool head can be knapped from some clay, as shown above, which can be used to cast low tier metals.

Recipe: precisionprospecting:anvil/metal/prospector_hammer/wrought_iron

A Prospector's Hammer Head can also be smithed out of a double ingot of any tool metal. Craft the head with a stick to make a Prospector's Hammer
