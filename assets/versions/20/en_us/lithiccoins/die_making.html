Die Making

The most important item for minting coins is a Top Die, as this is the part determining what the coin will depict.

The first step to making a Top Die is knapping your desired motive into Beeswax. This will require 4 pieces of Beeswax for any of the available stamp types.

Once you have crafted a Wax Die, you can turn it into an Unfired Die Mold.

Yellow White****

By heating the Wax Die, you get a Die Mold with your chosen stamp. This works like any other mold, needing to be filled with one ingot of liquid metal.

Tier I

Far easier to make is the Bottom Die. You can use any tool-grade metal, like with the Top Die, but it can only be made at an anvil.
