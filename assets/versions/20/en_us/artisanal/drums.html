Drums

Item: #artisanal:metal/drums

Drums are special barrels that are needed to store corrosive fluids, that would dissolve wooden barrels.

Drums are needed to store Sulfuric Acid and Lemon Juice.

Drums are made like Barrels, but with metal sheets instead of lumber.

Drums can be made from any bronze, as well as from steel.

Blue steel drums can also hold lava.

Recipe: artisanal:barrel/lava_garbage_disposal
