Fruit baskets

Item: lithicaddon:fruit_basket

Ever tried picking fruit from your fruit tree only to not be able to reach all the leaves? Well do I have a solution for you. Introducing the fruit basket, crafted with a bit of straw you can use it to right click a branch to shake the tree and drop a bunch of the fruits, collecting in the basket.

Be careful though as there is a 1 in 20 chance the branch snaps. This can be disabled in the config

Theres a secret if you right click on the fruit basket in your inventory with a powerful queen bee
