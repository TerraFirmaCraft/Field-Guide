Jar and Jam changes

No more ripping lids off

If you've ever been concerned about not being able to jam your fruits properly because of a lack of tin then fear not for this mod add two new types of jar lids.
An aluminium lid as well as a stainless steel lid. As well this mod changes the unsealing recipes to allow for a chance for the lids to be given back based on the metal the lid was made of.

  • 25% chance to get tin lids back
  • 50% chance to get aluminium lids back, and
  • 75% chance to get stainless steel lids backNow this also changes how you make jam in the first place, instead of needing an empty jar with lid to pick up the jam from the pot, you use an empty jar and then put a lid on afterwards. This also works with the vat
