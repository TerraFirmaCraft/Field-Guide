Dyeing Ceramics

Ceramic Vessels and Large Vessels can now be dyed using liquid dye, without a barrel.

In order to do this, fill a Ceramic Jug (or another liquid container) with at least 100 mb of liquid dye, and craft it with the item you want to dye.

Recipe: bsa:crafting/ceramic/magenta_unfired_vessel_liquid_dye

Recipe: bsa:crafting/ceramic/red_unfired_large_vessel_liquid_dye

Also, Jugs and Pots can be dyed, in all the same ways that other ceramics can be.

Recipe: bsa:crafting/ceramic/pot/unfired/black_liquid_dye
