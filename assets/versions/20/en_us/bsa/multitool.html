Multitools

Want to pre-knap a tool head before knowing what it'll be? Try a multitool! This stone tool head can be crafted into any tool, depending on the orientation of the stick and the multitool head in the crafting grid.

Tools crafted using a multitool head cannot also have a tool binding. A tool crafted with a multitool head is usually built to fulfill a specific need, and rarely lasts much longer once that need is met.

A multitool head can also be crafted into a knife head. Unlike other tools crafted with multitool heads, knives can have bindings like normal.
