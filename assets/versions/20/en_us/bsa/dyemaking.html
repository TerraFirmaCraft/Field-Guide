Dyemaking

In addition to boiling solid dye in water to make liquid dye, you can now boil the flowers from which solid dye is made, directly.

This results in less liquid dye than boiling solid dye would—only 100 mb per flower inserted.

The pot must also be at least
Bright Red during the boiling process.
