Pasta

There are two types of Noodles: Egg and Rice. Rice flour, maize flour, salt, and 1000 mB of milk together in a Mixing Bowl makes Rice Noodles. Flour, an egg, salt, and 1000 mB of milk makes Egg Noodles.

Lasagna can be made directly from egg noodles with Tomato Sauce, cooked meat, and oregano, which can then be baked in an Oven.

Pasta is then completed by boiling it in water. For either kind of noodles, it must be retrieved from the pot by clicking with a Bowl.

Crafting cooked egg noodles (pasta) with tomato sauce makes delicious pasta with tomato sauce!
