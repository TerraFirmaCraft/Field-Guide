Armor Trims

Armor trims are produced with a Trim Material and a Smithing Template. In TFC, all gems, along with Sterling Silver, Gold, Rose Gold, and Bismuth, can be used as trim materials. Armor trims are purely cosmetic.

The smithing table can be used to apply armor trims to TFC armor.
