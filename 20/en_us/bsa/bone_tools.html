Bone Tools

Bone can now be knapped into tools, just as rocks can.

Recipe: bsa:bone_knapping/fish_hook

Bone Fish Hooks can be knapped from bones, and can then be crafted into Bone Fishing Rods.

Recipe: bsa:bone_knapping/needle

Bone Needles are also knapped from bone and can be used to sew hides together.

Bone Needles can also be crafted from Sabertooth Fangs, which are dropped by Sabertooths.

Recipe: bsa:bone_knapping/bone/arrowhead/4_7_0_0

Bones can also be knapped into Arrowheads.
