Pizza

To make Pizza, mix Dough, Salt, Basil Leaves, and 100 mB of oil in a Mixing Bowl. Oil can be olive oil, or soybean oil (the product of soybean paste sealed in a barrel of water).

4

You'll need some shredded cheese to start with.

To make Tomato Sauce, boil a tomato, salt, garlic, and water all together in a pot. Alternatively, using a Vat, boil the crafted version of those ingredients, also known as Tomato Sauce Mix.

Pizza is made from 1-3 of vegetables, cooked meats, or fruits, one shredded cheese, and one tomato sauce.
