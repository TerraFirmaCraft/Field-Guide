Burritos and Tacos

The journey of making a Tortilla is a long process, but rewarding. Start with Maize Grain. Boil it in a pot of Limewater, and seal it in a barrel of water to make Nixtamal. Then crush it in a Quern to make Masa Flour.

Crafting the Masa Flour with a bucket of water makes Masa, the dough of a tortilla. This can be heated to make a Corn Tortilla. Baking a Tortilla in an oven makes a Taco Shell.

5

A tomato, cilantro, salt, and a knife will yield you Salsa.

The Burrito is made from cooked meat, Shredded Cheese, Tomato Sauce, a vegetable, and Salsa.

The Taco is made from the same ingredients, except with a Taco Shell.
