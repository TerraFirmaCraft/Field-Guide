Armor Trims

Armor trims are produced with a Trim Material and a Smithing Template. In TFC, all gems, along with Silver, Sterling Silver, Gold, Rose Gold, and Bismuth, can be used as trim materials. Armor trims are purely cosmetic.

Рецепт: minecraft:smithing_table

The smithing table can be used to apply armor trims to TFC armor.
